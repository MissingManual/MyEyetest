Is it flickering in your eyes?

Yes, and you might expect a very fast running graphics application chasing small black circles arround. In fact that is not the case and you are just looking at a static picture, but your brain is playing tricks with you.

If you do not believe print out the page and you will have still the same sensation.

The program allows you to play a bit arround with the parameters as to optimize the effect.

All values are taken as absolute values.
